How Does a Seed Ganesha Work? A Complete Guide to Eco-Friendly Ganesh Chaturthi

Bring Home Bappa, Give Back to Nature

Ganesh Chaturthi is a celebration of faith, devotion, and new beginnings. With increasing awareness about protecting our environment, many families are choosing Seed Ganesha idols as a sustainable way to celebrate while preserving tradition.

At Div Mangal Murtis, every Seed Ganesha hamper includes everything you need for a complete eco-friendly experience:

  • A handcrafted Seed Ganesha idol
  • Cocopeat
  • Plant Grow Bag
  • Natural Husk
  • Organic Fertiliser
  • Premium Quality Seeds

Step 1 – Perform the Pooja

Celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi just as you normally would. Our Seed Ganesha is handcrafted using natural, eco-friendly materials that are safe for the environment.

Step 2 – Immerse the Idol

Place the idol in a bucket or tub filled with clean water. Unlike traditional idols, the Seed Ganesha begins dissolving naturally and generally immerses completely within 24 hours.

Step 3 – Prepare the Pot

Mix the provided cocopeat, husk, and organic fertiliser inside the grow bag or a flower pot.

Step 4 – Plant the Seeds

Once the idol has dissolved, place the provided seeds into the prepared soil mixture.

Step 5 – Water with the Immersion Water

Instead of discarding the immersion water, use it to water the planted seeds for the next 7-8 days. This allows your Bappa to become a part of your growing plant in a completely natural way.

Why Choose a Seed Ganesha?

  • 100% Eco-Friendly
  • Naturally Dissolves in Water
  • Supports Sustainable Celebrations
  • Creates a Beautiful Plant After Visarjan
  • Safe for the Environment

Celebrate with Purpose

Choosing a Seed Ganesha in Bangalore is more than an eco-friendly decision—it's a meaningful way to continue Bappa's blessings through nature. At Div Mangal Murtis, we're proud to help families celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi responsibly while preserving the beauty of our traditions.

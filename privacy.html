Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25/06/2026

At Div Mangal Murtis, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit our website or place an order with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Billing & Delivery Address
  • PIN Code
  • Payment Information (processed securely through third-party payment providers)
  • Order History
  • Messages or enquiries submitted through our website, WhatsApp, email, or social media.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Contact you regarding your purchase.
  • Respond to your enquiries.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Share order updates and delivery information.
  • Improve our products, website, and services.
  • Send promotional offers, festive updates, or new product launches (only if you have opted to receive them).

3. Payment Security

We do not store your debit card, credit card, UPI PIN, CVV, or online banking credentials. Payments are processed securely through trusted third-party payment gateways.

4. Information Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your information may be shared only with:

  • Courier and logistics partners for order delivery.
  • Payment gateway providers for payment processing.
  • Government or legal authorities when required by applicable law.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, remember preferences, analyze website traffic, and enhance website performance.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some website features may not function properly.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. While we strive to maintain the highest level of security, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure.

7. Marketing Communications

With your consent, we may send you promotional messages, festive offers, and product updates through email, SMS, or WhatsApp. You may opt out of these communications at any time by contacting us or using the unsubscribe option where available.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, including Instagram, WhatsApp, or payment providers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external websites.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18 without appropriate parental or guardian consent.

10. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Update your contact details.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable legal and business requirements.

To make any such request, please contact us using the details provided below.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business, legal requirements, or services. Any updates will be published on this page with the revised effective date.

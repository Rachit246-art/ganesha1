About Our Idols
What materials do you use for your Ganesha idols?

We use natural Shadu clay sourced from the Godavari riverbed — the same material traditional artisans have used for centuries. Our paints are 100% plant-based and mineral pigments, free of toxic chemicals. We never use Plaster of Paris (PoP), synthetic paints, or harmful dyes.

Are your idols truly eco-friendly? Will they dissolve in water?

Absolutely. Natural Shadu clay dissolves completely in water within 1–3 days depending on idol size. Our plant-based colours leave no chemical residue. We also provide guidance on proper Visarjan in a bucket of water (tank immersion) rather than polluting rivers or lakes — a small step that makes a big difference.

What sizes are available?

We craft idols from 6 inches (desktop size) all the way up to 12 feet (for large mandals and public pandals). Our most popular home sizes are 1.5ft, 2ft, and 3ft. Custom sizes are available on request — just tell us your space dimensions and we will advise accordingly.

Do you follow traditional shilpashastra proportions?

Yes. Every idol is sculpted following the Dashabhuja Ganapati proportions as described in ancient texts. The facial features, hand mudras, ornament placements, and vahana ratios are all carefully observed. We believe a divinely proportioned idol invites positive energy into your home.

Custom Orders
Can I order a fully custom Ganesha — my own design?

Yes, this is one of our most beloved services. Share a sketch, mood board, photograph, or even a rough description and our design team will create a detailed clay sketch within 5–7 working days for your approval. We can incorporate family crests, Ganesh poses not in our standard catalogue, specific vahanas, and personalised ornament motifs.

How early should I place a custom order?

We recommend placing custom orders at least 8–12 weeks before your required date. For Ganesh Chaturthi (typically August–September), our books fill up quickly — many customers book in April and May. Standard catalogue orders can be placed 3–4 weeks in advance.

Can I see a progress update while my idol is being made?

Yes! We send 3 milestone photo updates — after the clay armature is complete, after the surface detailing, and after painting. You can request changes at the first two stages. This ensures you love every aspect of your idol before it ships.

Delivery and Packaging
How do you pack idols to prevent damage during shipping?

We use a 4-layer protection system: (1) soft muslin cloth wrap, (2) bubble wrap, (3) custom-cut foam mould that holds the exact idol shape, and (4) double-walled corrugated carton. Fragile parts like the trunk and crown receive individual padding. Every box is marked Fragile with orientation arrows.

Do you deliver outside India?

Yes. We ship internationally via DHL and FedEx to over 15 countries including the USA, UK, UAE, Canada, Australia, and Singapore. International shipping costs and delivery times are calculated at checkout. For large idols, we arrange sea freight with full insurance.

What if my idol arrives damaged?

Every shipment is insured. If your idol arrives damaged, photograph the packaging and idol and send it to us within 48 hours of delivery. We will either dispatch a replacement or send a restoration kit (for minor chips) at no additional cost. We stand 100% behind every piece we ship.

Restoration and Care
Can you restore an old or damaged Ganesha idol?

Yes — restoration is a service close to our heart. We have brought back idols that are 30–50 years old, repairing broken limbs, filling cracks, and repainting with natural colours that match the original style. Send us photographs of the idol and we will give you a free assessment and quote within 2 working days.

How do I care for my idol between festivals?

Store your idol in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. Wrap it in a soft cloth (muslin or cotton). Avoid plastic wrapping as it traps humidity. Do not wash with water — dust gently with a soft, dry brush. For annual touch-ups or repainting, contact us before the next festival season.

Pricing and Payment
What is the price range for your idols?

Our standard eco-friendly idols start from Rs. 1,200 for desktop sizes. Custom sculpted pieces begin at Rs. 5,000 and can go up depending on size and complexity. Large festival idols (4ft+) are quoted on a project basis. Contact us for a personalised quote — we offer options for every budget without compromising quality.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, all major credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. For international orders, we accept PayPal and wire transfers. A 50% advance is required to commence custom orders, with the balance due before dispatch.

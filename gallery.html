From Clay to Your Home

Every idol goes through our meticulous 4-stage creation process — from raw earth to a finished divine form ready for your puja.

01

Consultation

We discuss your requirements — size, pose, material, and occasion — to draft the perfect creative brief.

02

Sculpting

Master artisans hand-sculpt using premium Shadu clay, adhering to traditional shilpashastra proportions.

03

Finishing and Painting

Natural mineral pigments and gold accents are applied by hand, giving each piece a vibrant, lasting finish.

04

Safe Delivery

Custom foam-fitted packaging ensures your idol arrives in perfect condition anywhere in India.

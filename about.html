Our Heritage

A Legacy Carved
in Devotion

Div Mangal Murtis was founded with a single, unwavering belief — that every Ganesha idol should carry within it the soul of its maker. Over the last 25 years, our master artisans have hand-carved thousands of idols, each one a unique expression of devotion, beauty, and skilled craftsmanship.

We began in a small workshop in Pune, where our founder Shri Mangal Das first learned the ancient art of murti-making from his grandfather. That workshop grew — not through compromise, but through a relentless commitment to quality and an ever-deepening love for the craft.

Today, Div Mangal Murtis serves devotees across India and internationally, yet every idol still passes through the same careful hands and the same heartfelt quality check that has defined us from day one.

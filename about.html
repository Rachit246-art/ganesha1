About Us

At Div Mangal Murtis, we believe every Ganesh Chaturthi begins with a beautiful welcome for Bappa. What started in 2025 as a passion project by a Marathi artist and her Kannada husband has grown into a trusted destination for premium Ganesha idols in Bangalore.

Rooted in tradition, we source our exclusive Solapur antique collections from skilled artisans and carefully hand-finish every murti with precision and devotion. Alongside our antique masterpieces, we also offer eco-friendly Seed Ganeshas and festive decor, ensuring every family finds the perfect Bappa for their home.

Our mission has always been simple to offer premium craftsmanship at honest prices while making the experience effortless through safe packaging, doorstep delivery, and personalized service. Every murti we create is more than a work of art; it is the beginning of countless memories, prayers, and celebrations shared with loved ones.

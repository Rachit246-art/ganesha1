[ COMMON QUERIES ]

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our handcrafted Ganeshas, eco-friendly materials, and safe delivery.

Are your Ganesha idols truly 100% eco-friendly?

Yes! We use only natural Shadu clay sourced from riverbeds and water-soluble plant-based colors. They dissolve completely in water without harming marine life or leaving any toxic residue.

How does the safe delivery process work?

Each murti is carefully packaged in custom foam-fitted wooden crates designed specifically for its dimensions. We provide fully insured transit so your divine idol arrives safely and securely at your doorstep.

Can I customize my Ganesha idol?

Absolutely. Since every piece is individually hand-sculpted by our master artisans, we can accommodate custom requests for posture, colors, sizes, and specific traditional detailing to perfectly match your devotion.

What is a Seed Ganesha?

A Seed Ganesha is an eco-friendly idol that contains organic plant seeds inside. After the festival, instead of immersing it in a river, you can place it in a pot at home. As it dissolves, the seeds take root, growing into a beautiful plant that keeps the divine blessing in your home forever.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What material are the Solapur antique idols made of?

Our Solapur antique Ganpati murtis are crafted using a traditional blend of chalk, natural husk, and a proprietary bonding material that enhances strength and durability. They are finished with eco-friendly paints and typically take 5-7 weeks to fully immerse in water.

2. How long does a Seed Ganesha take to immerse?

Our eco-friendly Seed Ganesha idols dissolve completely in approximately 24 hours. Once immersed, simply plant the included cocopeat and seeds in the provided pot, then use the immersion water to nourish the plant for the next 7-8 days. This creates a beautiful, 100% natural continuation of your Ganesh Chaturthi celebration.

3. Is home delivery free?

We offer free home delivery within the 560001 pincode in Bangalore. For all other locations in Bangalore, across India, and internationally, delivery is available at an additional shipping charge. Every murti is securely packed to ensure it reaches you safely.

4. Can I visit your store or warehouse?

Yes, you're welcome to visit our warehouse to explore our Ganpati collections and book your preferred murti. As we are working professionals and visits are by arrangement, we kindly request that you schedule a prior appointment before visiting.
